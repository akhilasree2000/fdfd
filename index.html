Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is one of the greatest cricketers in the history of the sport and a national icon in India. Born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, Maharashtra, he is often referred to as the "God of Cricket." Tendulkar's illustrious career spanned over two decades, during which he set numerous records and inspired millions worldwide.

  • Played international cricket for 24 years (1989–2013)
  • First player to score 100 international centuries.
  • Accumulated 34,357 runs across all formats of international cricket, the most by any player.
